About LabCurate

LabCurate searches across the accreditation schedules of 1,524 UKAS-accredited testing and calibration laboratories, covering 14,298 individual capabilities. We match your requirements against 77,000 international testing standards to advise on what testing you need and who can do it.

Data source: Capability data is extracted from publicly available UKAS accreditation schedules. Standards data from ISO Open Data. Lab accreditation status should always be verified at ukas.com.
